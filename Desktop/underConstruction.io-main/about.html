About This application

Making the experience of tracking your DPDHL parcels a little less frustrating

I am a receptionist at DHL Express. For years I have noticed one common gripe that customers have, they know that their shipment is coming with DHL but not what service.

Through years of experience I can now deduce what service a shipment is coming with on sight. However, customer often can't and neither should they have to.

With that in mind I set out to learn how to build an application designed to help customers with that problem and direct them to the correct servic first time.
